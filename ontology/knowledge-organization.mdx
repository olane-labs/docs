### Entities

Every meaningful thing in your system — a person, tool, project, service, concept — becomes an **entity** in the ontology. Entities are what the system builds its understanding around.

### Dependencies

The ontology maps how entities relate to and depend on each other — which services call which, what relies on what, who owns what, where a change in one place ripples to others.

This dependency awareness is what enables long-term planning. Instead of reasoning about isolated components, the system can trace impact across the full graph and surface risks before they become problems.
